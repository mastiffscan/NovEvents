#MastiffsCanUnwrapPotential Gift Exchange 2025

Celebrate the Season — Unwrap Potential

Celebrate the season with Mastiff love and gifts that support all the amazing things our dogs can do. This year’s theme:
#MastiffsCan Unwrap Potential

How to Participate

  • Registration: October 22 – November 20, 2025
  • Gift value limit: $25 including shipping
  • Matches announced: First week of December
  • Shipping deadline: December 15, 2025
  • Eligibility: Must have participated in one of the virtual walks

Whether your Mastiff is earning titles, learning new skills, logging Fit Dog miles, or simply working on being their best self—this exchange is about encouragement, celebration, and community fun.

Gift Theme Ideas

Gifts should be useful, thoughtful, and training-friendly—with a touch of seasonal spirit.

  • Training books or workbooks (obedience, fitness, teamwork, trick training)
  • A fun leash—something with flair yet practical
  • Interactive feeders or enrichment tools
  • A small ornament or keepsake to celebrate a 2025 accomplishment
  • Something for the handler that marks a milestone or supports training

Please include a short note about your Mastiff’s training journey or a favorite accomplishment.

If Sending Through Amazon or Direct Ship

If your gift ships directly (such as via Amazon), please message your match separately. Introduce yourself and share something about your Mastiff’s journey—this connection keeps the exchange personal, even if the box looks plain.

Multiple Pets?

You have two options:

  1. Submit one form for all pets (one match total)
  2. Submit individual forms for each pet (a separate match for each)

Want to Help?

You can sign up as a backup gifter—someone who helps out if a participant can’t send their gift. Backup gifters make the experience positive for everyone.

Important Participation Disclaimer

This exchange is voluntary and community-led, facilitated by the MCOA Working Dog Committee. We cannot guarantee that every participant will follow through.

  • You may not receive a gift, though we hope all participants do.
  • Organizers are not responsible for lost, delayed, or missing packages.
  • If you sign up and do not send a gift, you may not participate in future exchanges.

If you do not receive a gift by January 1, 2026, email MastiffsCanEvent@gmail.com and we will do our best to assist.
