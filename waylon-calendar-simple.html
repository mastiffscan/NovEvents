Calendar Contest & Raffle • November 1–30, 2025

Waylon’s November Calendar Contest & Raffle

Waylon’s yearly calendar tradition is more than just fun photos—it’s his way of giving back. Through his calendars, Waylon has raised over $13,000 in just two years for Lucie’s Mastiff Mission, helping Mastiffs in need. To celebrate that spirit, Waylon and Carrie Klaiber are sponsoring a special Raffle Contest—because #MastiffsCan inspire creativity, give back, and bring people together.

Requirement Participants must be members of the #MastiffsCan Facebook Group

How to Enter the Calendar Contest

  1. 1

    Create your calendar

    Make a 12‑photo calendar—any theme you like.

  2. 2

    Get evaluated (free)

    Submit your calendar to a Do More With Your Dog! evaluator to earn the Calendar Shoot Title.

    Get an Evaluator
  3. 3

    You’re entered

    Complete your Calendar Shoot evaluation between November 1–30, 2025 and you’re automatically in Waylon’s raffle.

Evaluations are always free.

Make it official (optional): after approval, submit to Do More With Your Dog! for the official Calendar Shoot Title ($25 submission fee).

The title is worth 10 points in Category 4 toward the Mastiff Working Dog Awards. You can also include it in your November Intentions.

Tips
  • Pick a theme that shows your dog’s personality.
  • Use natural light and keep backgrounds simple.
  • Think variety—close‑ups, action shots, quiet moments.

Remember: You must be a member of the #MastiffsCan Facebook Group.

Dates to Remember

  • Contest Window: Nov 1–Nov 30, 2025
  • Raffle Selection: Waylon selects from all evaluated entries
  • Once you finish, your DMWYD evaluator will post in the group to celebrate—add your calendar to inspire others.

Raffle Prize

One entrant will receive Waylon’s 2026 Calendar—a symbol of his yearly tradition that supports Mastiff rescue and spreads joy year‑round.
