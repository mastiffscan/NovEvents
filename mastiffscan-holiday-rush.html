BTC-24 Challenge – An Example of an Ultra-Fast 12-Trick Sequence for Mastiffs

Goal: Complete 12 tricks in under 24 seconds.

Strategy: Use micro-movements, smooth body transitions, and paired tricks that chain naturally with zero wasted motion.

Suggested 12-Trick Flow (zero wasted motion)

  1. Sit – Start position; easy and sets up the next trick.
  2. Down – Quick, clean transition from sit.
  3. Chin Rest / Head Down – From down, just a tiny movement; very fast.
  4. Sit Up – Pop back into a sit (optional: “sit pretty” if your Mastiff can do it safely).
  5. Stand – From sit to stand keeps momentum going.
  6. Spin (Left) – Immediate motion from standing.
  7. Spin (Right) – Mirrors the first spin; fast and flashy.
  8. Hand Touch (Left Hand) – Dog targets your hand from the stand/spin position.
  9. Hand Touch (Right Hand) – Mirror of the previous trick; no repositioning.
  10. Paw/Shake (Left) – Lift paw; dog is already in perfect position.
  11. Paw/Shake (Right) – Mirrors the left; fast and symmetrical.
  12. Bow – Dramatic finish that flows naturally from a standing position.

Why This Sequence Works

  • No props required.
  • No repositioning—the dog remains directly in front of you.
  • Smooth transitions between sit, down, stand, and motion.
  • Paired and mirrored tricks reduce cognitive load for the dog.
  • High-certainty behaviors that work well for large breeds.
  • Avoids slower, space-consuming tricks like rollovers and figure-8 leg weaves.

Estimated Time for a Well-Trained Mastiff

With clean execution and a confident handler cue rhythm, 17–23 seconds is realistically achievable. A Mastiff breaking the BTC-24 barrier is absolutely within reach using this style of sequence.
